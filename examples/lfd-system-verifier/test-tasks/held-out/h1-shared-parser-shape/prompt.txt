All 5 adapter parsers must produce the same 8-key shared
shape on identical empty input. This proves the parser
contract is uniform across adapters.

The 5 parsers are:
- cline-orchestration/scripts/parse_cline_output.py
- claude-code-orchestration/scripts/parse_claude_output.py
- codex-orchestration/scripts/parse_codex_output.py
- hermes-agent-orchestration/scripts/parse_hermes_output.py
- opencode-orchestration/scripts/parse_opencode_output.py

For each: invoke with an empty path, assert the 8 keys are
present and have the right types.
